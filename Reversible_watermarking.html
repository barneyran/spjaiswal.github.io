Reversible Watermarking

Objective

Reversible watermarking received much popularity in recent years because of copyright protection of data. Reversible watermarking means embedding a specific information into the cover media in such a way that it can recover the original cover media at the decoder. Reversible watermarking is useful for important media, such as medical and military image, because it need to recover completely without any loss. Reversible watermarking are also useful in other applications such as image and video coding.
